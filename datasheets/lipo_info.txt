////////////////////////////////////////////////////
// Multistar High Capacity 16000mAh LiPo Battery
////////////////////////////////////////////////////

Capacity: 16000mAh (16Ah)
Configuration: 6S2P (6 cells in series, 2 cells in parallel)
Voltage: 22.2V
Continuous Discharge Rate: 10C (160A)
Peak Discharge Rate (10 sec): 20C (320A)
Weight: 1920g (1.92kg)
Dimensions: 173mm x 74mm x 68mm
Charge Plug: JST-XH
Discharge Plug: XT90

////////////////////////////////////////////////////
// Charging Information
////////////////////////////////////////////////////

Standard Charge Rate: 1C (16A)
Actual Charge Rate: 0.125C (2A)

Fully Charged: 25.2V (4.2V per cell)
Nominal Voltage: 22.2V (3.7V per cell)
Safe Minimum Voltage: 19.8V (3.3V per cell)
Absolute Minimum Voltage (Avoid): 18.0V (3.0V per cell)

1. Connect the JST-XH (6S) Charge Plug
2. Connect the XT90 Discharge Plug
3. Plug in and Power the Charging Device
4. Select LiPo Battery
5. Change to LiPo Balance Mode
6. Set Charge Rate to 2A
7. Hold "Enter" to Confirm
8. Press "Enter" to Start Charging
